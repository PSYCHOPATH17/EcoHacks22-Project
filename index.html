The Problem



Environmental pollution is one of the leading causes of detriment to this world. Types of pollution present in the modern-day environment are air pollution, ocean pollution, and land pollution. Each of these types of pollutant types cause their own dents in nature. An article conspired by www.mass.gov states, "...Pollution can damage crops and trees in a variety of ways. Ground-level ozone can lead to reductions in agricultural crop and commercial forest yields, reduced growth and survivability of tree seedlings, and increased plant susceptibility to disease, pests and other environmental stresses (such as harsh weather)."

The Solution

We plan to diminish the derogatory effects of pollution with our action plan. This consists of little effects that are planned to be inaugurated into the world, from recycling, to promoting the occupancy of EVehicles and more.

  • EVehicle Education

    Run campaigns and spread news to educate civilians on the benefits of EVehicles.

  • Pick Up

    Form groups with volunteers to travel to civilized areas and pick up trash.

  • Recycle

    Reduce the amount of plastic in the oceans by simply recycling instead of dumping everything in the trash.

  • Plant Trees

    Associate with organizations and volunteers to plant trees. #TeamTrees

  • Bicycles

    Encourage people to travel by bicycles. Inaugurate this by using bicycles yourself.
